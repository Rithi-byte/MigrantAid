
# 🌍 MigrantAid

**Empowering migrant workers through AI-driven support, learning, and community connections.**

---

## 📌 Overview

MigrantAid is a digital platform built to address the real-world challenges faced by migrant workers — including misinformation, language barriers, and lack of access to legal, emotional, and upskilling support. Through an intuitive interface and an AI-powered chatbot, the platform delivers 24/7 help, verified resources, and a bridge to trusted networks.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Migrant workers often face:
- ❌ No centralized help or guidance
- ❌ Language and communication barriers
- ❌ Misinformation and legal confusion
- ❌ Limited access to verified skill-building resources and support

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To develop a **unified support system** that:
- Provides **verified upskilling courses**
- Offers a **chatbot-based 24/7 helpline**
- Shares **real-time news** and **migrant rights updates**
- Connects users to **NGOs and legal assistance**
